Hi there, I'm Ashkan

I'm a second-year PhD student at the University of Trento and Fondazione Bruno Kessler (FBK), working under the supervision of Fabio Remondino in the 3DOM research unit at FBK. My research focuses on open-vocabulary point cloud segmentation, particularly using photogrammetric data.

I'm fascinated by bringing the power of 2D vision-language models into the 3D world, making 3D understanding more flexible and scalable through training-free segmentation, zero-shot learning, and models that just get it without being spoon-fed. I love the idea of machines that can instantly make sense of a 3D scene!

Previously, I obtained my MS in Computer Science from the University of Trento. Outside of research, I enjoy climbing, hiking, and mountains.
